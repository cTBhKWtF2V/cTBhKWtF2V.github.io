Living

Accommodation

The New School offers a variety of student housing options, but we do not guarantee university housing for all students. Visiting and exchange students are welcome to apply for housing as soon as they are admitted to Parsons.

Health insurance

Students going to the U.S. may be required to purchase compulsory health insurance as instructed by the host institution as well as purchasing travel insurance from Australia.

Living cost

Visa
